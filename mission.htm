Strategic Strike/Escort:
Scenario:
Attacking fleet against Defending fleet with a specific Target ship and escort ships
Team 1: Attacker Objective-
Destroy the Target ship
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Prevent the Target ship from being destroyed, destroy X points worth of Attacker fleet
 Station Siege:
Scenario:
Target is a Station
Team 1: Attacker Objective-
Destroy the Target station
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Prevent the Target station from being destroyed
 Objective Raid:
Scenario:
Attacking fleet interdicts Defending fleet warp for X rounds and targets a specific Target ship
Team 1: Attacker Objective-
Destroy the Target ship
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Prevent the Target ship from being destroyed until warp capable afer X rounds
Objective Seizure:
Scenario:
Attacking fleet against Defending fleet with a specific Target station to capture
Team 1: Attacker Objective-
Capture the Target station and hold at end of battle
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Prevent the Target station from being captured at the end of the battle
 Timed Raid:
Scenario:
Attacking fleet against Defending fleet with a specific Target station to capture, heavy Defender reinforcements in route to arrive later in battle
Team 1: Attacker Objective-
Capture the Target station (do not have to hold)
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Prevent the Target station from being captured at all or destroyed
 Timed Intercept:
Scenario:
Attacking fleet intercepts Disabled Target ship to capture, heavy Defender reinforcements in route to arrive later in battle
Team 1: Attacker Objective-
Capture the Target ship and exit the battle and prevent from being destroyed
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Prevent the Target ship from leaving the battle or being destroyed
Disputed Annex:
Scenario:
2 Attacking fleets of equal point value attempt to capture the same Target Station
Team 1: Target Station Objective-
Prevent self destruction until capture, destroy X points worth of all enemy ships
Team 2: Objective-
Capture the Target station and hold to end of battle, and prevent it from being destroyed
Team 3: Objective-
Capture the Target station and hold to end of battle, and prevent it from being destroyed
Surprise Recon:
Scenario:
Intruding team attempts to scan battlefield and escape with recon information against superior defensive forces
Team 1: Intruder Objective-
Escape the battle with at least 1 ship after X rounds have passed
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Prevent any Intruder from leaving the battle after X rounds
 Surprise Strike:
Scenario:
Cloaked attacking fleet attempts to destroy Target ship while Defender conditioning is delayed 1 round
Team 1: Build-
All ships with cloaking devices that condition cloaked
Team 1: Intruder Objective-
Destroy the Target ship
Team 2: Defender Objective-
Target ship takes last slot of order. Prevent the Target ship from being destroyed
