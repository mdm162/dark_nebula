Planet Tables - Planetary Setup Planetary Structures
Field Description
Name Standard name for planet
Team Team that controller currently aligned with
Controller Player currently in control of planet
Type Type of planetoid:
Jovian: Large, gaseous planetoid with no surface, gas giant. May give bonus production to orbital gas extracters.
Terrestrial: Medium grade, liquid core planet with variable compositions. Excellent size and orbital factors allow for high slot space.
Metallic: Heavy, dense planetoid with high compositions of heavy metals. May give bonus production to mining stations.
Class Type of planetary atmosphere:
Class V: Extremely thick outer atmosphere, high cloud density, violent
Class L: Thick atmosphere, total cloud cover, moderate
Class M: Moderate atmosphere, partial cloud cover, temperate
Class H: Thin atmosphere, radioactive, high temperate variation
Class A: No atmosphere, no gas activity, no temperature moderation
Mineral Level Level of quality and quantity within the planet's mineral composition. These levels directly affect the output of mineral processing stations. Higher with rich, metallic planetoids with little atmosphere.
Gas Level Level of purity and volume within the planet's atmospheric composition. These levels directly affect the output of gas processing stations. Higher with gaseous, jovian planetoids with thick atmospheres.
Labor Level Productivity and population of the planet's labor pool. These levels directly affect the output of research labs, and indirectly help the output of all structures within the planet, by increasing their productivity and output levels. Higher with temperate enviroments and living space.
Surface Slots Number of open land units available for buildings.
Orbital Slots Number of open orbital lanes available for orbitals.
Energy Current level of power generated by the planet's power grid for use by its structures. This level directly affects the output of all structures, up to 100% of their normal output.
Mineral Production Current mineral output of the planet per turn. Based on Mining Stations * Mineral Level at the current percentage of planetary energy.
Gas Production Current gas output of the planet per turn. Based on Gas Stations * Gas Level at the current percentage of planetary energy.
Parts Production Current parts output of the planet per turn. Based on Manufacturing Facilities * (Mineral Level + Gas Level) at the current percentage of planetary energy
Research Production Current research output of the planet per turn. Based on Research Labs * Labor Level at the current percentage of planetary energy.
