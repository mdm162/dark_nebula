Weapon Types-
Warhead:
Can be built as a warhead charge and armed on a missile or torpedo for detonation. Warheads have decent power, and typically excellent modifiers, making them better support weapons for other types.
Array:
Designed as a large array that may even have multiple output emittors that must work together to fire. Arrays usually fire slow but extremely powerful rounds.
Beam:
Built into a beam weapon that fires from large immobile emittors, firing medium level shots with decent power. Many beam weapons have omnidirectional emission, so they can be aimed very easily and accurately.
Cannon:
Weapon cannon are smaller, fast firing variants that are designed to saturate more than direct fire attack. Cannon can be pivoted, and deliver high speed 'pulses' of energy rather than sustained beams.
Battery:
Best resembling 'turrets', batteries are designed purely for high rate of fire. Weapon batteries usually enjoy high saturation power at a cost to direct damage, firing a continuous string of minute energy 'bolts'.
